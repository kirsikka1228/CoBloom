# Product

## Register

product

## Users

CoBloom serves individual learners, students, and knowledge workers who collect Markdown notes and want to turn them into a personal AI-assisted knowledge base. Users arrive to record learning material, ask questions against their own notes, review history, and inspect relationships between concepts.

## Product Purpose

CoBloom combines note management, RAG question answering, AI summaries, recommendations, and a lightweight knowledge graph. Success means users can sign in, manage their private knowledge base, retrieve reliable answers from their own material, and navigate related notes without losing trust in data separation or workflow clarity.

## Brand Personality

Young, handmade, exploratory. The product should feel like a creative learning studio rather than an enterprise admin console, while the authenticated tools remain clear and dependable.

## Anti-references

Avoid conventional SaaS login pages, generic admin templates, polished corporate gradients, and form-first authentication screens that bury the CoBloom identity. Avoid redesigning brand lettering when an approved visual asset exists.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with the artifact: use real CoBloom visual assets as the strongest identity signal.
2. Keep authentication functional: login controls should serve the task without overpowering the entrance artwork.
3. Preserve creative texture: handmade collage and magazine-cutout energy are part of the product promise.
4. Respect existing systems: reuse the current Vue, Router, Pinia, Element Plus, and API conventions.
5. Protect trust: authentication state, redirects, and failure states must behave predictably.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

The interface should support keyboard operation, visible focus states, readable form labels, inline error feedback, and reduced-motion preferences. The primary login artwork remains decorative and functional through an accessible overlay control with an explicit label.
